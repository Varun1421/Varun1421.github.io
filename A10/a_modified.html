Chart Literacy Checklist

  1. Does the y-axis start at zero when appropriate?
  2. Has the chart cropped the time window to favor a narrative?
  3. Are labels, units, and data sources clearly shown?
  4. Does the title imply causation without sufficient evidence?
  5. Are important data points or contextual details omitted?
  6. Does the colour scheme bias the viewer toward a positive or negative interpretation?
  7. Does the metric’s direction match intuitive chart movement?
  8. Are labels or numbers inconsistent with the visual itself? (AI Red Flag)
  9. Are axis scales distorted, uneven, or mathematically inconsistent? (AI Red Flag)
  10. Does the chart include unsupported annotations, fake precision, or invented conclusions? (AI Red Flag)
